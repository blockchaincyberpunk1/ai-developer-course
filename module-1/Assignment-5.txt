AI in Popular Culture

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will watch a movie or TV series that features artificial intelligence or robots, such as "Blade Runner," "Ex Machina," or "Westworld." They will then write a review or analysis of how AI is portrayed in the selected media and discuss whether it aligns with real-world AI concepts and concerns. Additionally, students will share their thoughts on the potential impact of such portrayals on public perception. This assignment encourages students to explore the representation of AI in popular culture and its implications.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to AI in Popular Culture (15 minutes)

Provide an introduction to the assignment, explaining the significance of analyzing AI portrayals in popular culture and their potential influence on public perception.
Task 2: Media Selection (10 minutes)
2. Ask students to select a movie or TV series that features artificial intelligence or robots. Provide a list of suggested titles or allow students to choose their own.

Task 3: Media Review/Analysis (3 - 4 hours)
3. Instruct students to watch the selected movie or TV series carefully and take notes on how AI is portrayed. Then, write a review or analysis (approximately 800-1000 words) that includes the following components:

Introduction to the Media (10%):

Provide a brief introduction to the selected movie or TV series, including its plot and context.
AI Portrayal Analysis (40%):

Analyze how AI and AI-related themes are portrayed in the media. Consider aspects such as:
The capabilities and limitations of AI characters or technology
Ethical dilemmas presented
Human-AI interactions
Implications for society
Alignment with Real-World AI Concepts (30%):

Discuss whether the media's portrayal of AI aligns with real-world AI concepts, technologies, and concerns. Identify any accurate or misleading representations.
Impact on Public Perception (15%):

Share your thoughts on how the portrayal of AI in popular culture may influence public perception and attitudes toward AI. Consider potential positive and negative effects.
Conclusion (5%):

Summarize your analysis and reflect on the broader implications of media portrayals of AI.
Task 4: Submission (30 minutes - 1 hour)
4. Request students to submit their media reviews or analyses.

Task 5: Peer Review (1 hour)
5. Organize a peer review session where students exchange their reviews or analyses for feedback. Encourage constructive criticism and suggestions for improvement.

Task 6: Revision (30 minutes - 1 hour)
6. Based on the feedback received during the peer review, ask students to revise and refine their reviews or analyses.

Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their finalized media reviews or analyses.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Depth of analysis of AI portrayal in the selected media
Evaluation of alignment with real-world AI concepts and concerns
Thoughtful consideration of the impact on public perception
