Historical Overview of AI

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will research and write a brief report on the history of artificial intelligence (AI). They will explore key milestones, developments, and important figures in the field of AI. The assignment encourages students to understand how the concept of AI has evolved over time and its significance in contemporary society.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to the History of AI (15 minutes)

Introduce students to the significance of studying the history of AI and how it has shaped the field of artificial intelligence today.
Task 2: Research and Report (2 - 3 hours)
2. Assign students the task of researching and writing a brief report on the history of AI. The report should include the following components:

Introduction (10%):

Provide a brief introduction to the concept of artificial intelligence and its significance in today's world.
Historical Milestones (40%):

Summarize key milestones and developments in the history of AI. Include significant breakthroughs, inventions, or discoveries.
Highlight the work of important figures in the field, such as Alan Turing, John McCarthy, and others.
Evolution of AI (30%):

Discuss how the concept of AI has evolved over time, including shifts in goals, approaches, and challenges.
Explain the development of AI from rule-based systems to machine learning and deep learning.
Significance Today (15%):

Explain the current significance of AI in various domains, such as healthcare, finance, and automation.
Discuss the societal impact of AI and potential future developments.
Conclusion (5%):

Summarize the key takeaways from the history of AI and its relevance today.
Task 3: Submission (30 minutes - 1 hour)
3. Request students to submit their written reports on the history of AI.

Task 4: Peer Review (1 hour)
4. Organize a peer review session where students exchange their reports for feedback. Encourage constructive criticism and suggestions for improvement.

Task 5: Revision (30 minutes - 1 hour)
5. Based on the feedback received during the peer review, ask students to revise and refine their reports.

Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their finalized reports on the history of AI.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Clarity and completeness of the historical overview, including key milestones and figures.
Insightful analysis of the evolution of AI and its significance today.
