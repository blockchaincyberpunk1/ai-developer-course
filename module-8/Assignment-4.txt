Data Privacy and Regulations

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will explore data privacy regulations, including GDPR and CCPA, and their implications for AI projects. They will analyze a fictional AI project, identify potential data privacy concerns, and propose a comprehensive plan to ensure compliance with relevant data privacy regulations and protect user data.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to Data Privacy Regulations (1-2 hours)

Provide an overview of data privacy regulations such as GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act).
Explain the key principles and requirements of these regulations, emphasizing their importance in safeguarding user data.
Task 2: AI Project Analysis (2-3 hours)
3. Present a fictional AI project scenario to the students. Describe the nature of the project, its objectives, and the types of data it collects, processes, or stores.

Instruct students to analyze the project and identify potential data privacy concerns or risks. These concerns may relate to data collection, processing, storage, or user consent.
Task 3: Compliance Plan Proposal (3-4 hours)
5. Assign students the task of proposing a data privacy compliance plan for the fictional AI project.

In their plan, students should address:
Data collection and consent mechanisms.
Data storage and security measures.
Data processing and user rights (e.g., right to access, right to be forgotten).
Data breach response and notification procedures.
Appointing a Data Protection Officer (DPO) if necessary.
Task 4: Documentation and Justification (2-3 hours)
7. Encourage students to document their compliance plan, providing detailed explanations for each aspect of the plan.

Ask them to justify their choices by referencing specific clauses or requirements from GDPR, CCPA, or other relevant regulations.
Task 5: Presentation or Report (2-3 hours)
9. Require students to create a presentation or write a report summarizing their compliance plan, data privacy concerns identified in the AI project, and the justifications for their proposed measures.

Encourage them to include any visual aids, diagrams, or flowcharts to clarify their plan.
Grading Criteria:
The assignment will be assessed based on the following criteria:

The depth of analysis regarding data privacy concerns in the AI project.
The comprehensiveness and feasibility of the compliance plan.
The clarity and justifiability of choices based on regulations.
The quality of the presentation or report, including visual aids and documentation.
