AI Blog Post

Assignment Overview:
This assignment is designed to encourage students to explore trending AI topics and communicate their findings in a clear and engaging manner through a blog post. Students will select a relevant AI topic, research it thoroughly, and create a blog post that is accessible to a non-technical audience.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Topic Selection (1-2 hours)

Provide students with a list of trending AI topics or allow them to propose their own topics for approval. Ensure the topics are relevant to the course content and accessible to a general audience.
Task 2: Research and Content Creation (4-5 hours)
2. Instruct students to conduct research on their chosen topic. They should gather information from reputable sources, research papers, or news articles.

Ask students to create a well-structured blog post that includes the following:
An introduction that provides context and explains the importance of the topic.
An explanation of the topic in simple terms, avoiding technical jargon.
Real-world examples or use cases that illustrate the topic's relevance.
Practical insights, recommendations, or implications related to the topic.
Clear headings, subheadings, and visuals (images, diagrams) to enhance readability.
Proper citation and references for all sources used.
Task 3: Blog Post Sharing (Optional, 2-3 hours)
4. Encourage students to share their completed blog posts with the class during a presentation session. Alternatively, they can publish their posts on a platform of their choice, such as a personal blog or a social media platform.

Grading Criteria:
Evaluation will be based on the following criteria:

Clarity and accessibility of the blog post to a non-technical audience.
Depth of research and relevance of examples.
Practical insights and recommendations provided.
Proper citation and references.
Quality of presentation (if applicable).
